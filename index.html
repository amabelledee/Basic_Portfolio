About Me

I was born and raised in San Diego, CA, and I am the 3rd of 5 girls. My parents kept trying for a boy and couldn't quite get there. I graduated from the University of California, Riverside, with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. In junior high and high school, I was placed in a few computer science classes and learned HTML. While I have always had an interest in coding, I have never pursued a carrer in it until now. My goal is to create beautiful, user-friendly, websites that have strong search engine optimization (SEO), and assist companies with growing their business and optimizing their websites.
